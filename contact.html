Shipping & Delivery

We Deliver
Nationwide

From the Free State to every province — your Prestige Apostle shoes come to you.

Nationwide Delivery

We ship to all nine provinces — Gauteng, Western Cape, KwaZulu-Natal, Eastern Cape, Free State, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, North West, and Northern Cape. No matter where you are in South Africa, we reach you.

How to Order

Simply WhatsApp us on +27 72 413 7487. Tell us the shoe line you want (e.g. Apostles, Elders), your shoe size, and your delivery address. We'll confirm availability and share payment details.

Delivery Timeline

Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days. Delivery times vary by province — Free State and surrounding areas are usually faster. We will give you an estimated delivery window when you order.

Bulk & Corporate Orders

Need shoes for a group, event, or business? We accommodate bulk orders and corporate packages. Contact us on WhatsApp and we'll discuss quantity, pricing, and customisation options.

Please Note: Delivery fees may apply depending on your location and order size. We will always confirm the full cost — including delivery — before you commit to your order. No hidden charges. Ever.
