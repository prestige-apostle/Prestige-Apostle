The Origin
01

Where It
All Began

Prestige Apostle was founded in Bloemfontein, Free State — the heart of South Africa — with one clear vision: to dress the African gentleman with the dignity he deserves.

We watched professionals across the country settle for ordinary footwear that didn't reflect their excellence. We decided to change that. Every man who walks into a boardroom, a church, a wedding, or a formal occasion deserves shoes that speak before he does.

The Name
02

Why Apostle?
Why These Names?

Our six product lines — Apostles, Oversees, Elders, Priests, Deacons, and Brothers — are drawn from ranks of leadership, service, and honour. These are not just names. They are a framework of identity.

We believe every man occupies a role of significance. Whether you are an elder in your community, a brother to those around you, or a leader in your field — there is a Prestige Apostle shoe made for the authority you carry.

Wear your rank. Walk in your calling.

What We Stand For

Our Core
Values

Everything we do flows from three unbreakable commitments to our customers.

01
Uncompromising Quality

We use only premium leather and quality materials. Every pair is inspected before it leaves our hands. We would rather produce fewer shoes than compromise on the standard that defines us.

02
Identity & Purpose

Our lines are named with intention. We believe clothing and footwear carry meaning. When you put on a pair of Prestige Apostle shoes, you are putting on a mindset — one of purpose, leadership, and excellence.

03
Accessible Excellence

Premium does not have to be impossible to reach. We deliver nationwide across all nine provinces of South Africa, bringing world-class formal footwear directly to your door via a simple WhatsApp order.

Nationwide

We Deliver to
Every Province

From our base in Bloemfontein, Free State, Prestige Apostle delivers to all nine provinces of South Africa — Gauteng, Western Cape, KwaZulu-Natal, Eastern Cape, Limpopo, Mpumalanga, North West, and Northern Cape.

Ordering is simple. Reach out on WhatsApp, tell us your size, choose your line, and we handle the rest. No complicated checkouts. No long queues. Just premium shoes delivered to you.
